Electronics Component Guide

Resistor

Definition:

A resistor limits current in a circuit.

Formula

V = I × R

Capacitor

Definition:

Stores energy in electric field.

Formula

XC = 1 / (2πfC)

Inductor

Definition:

Stores energy in magnetic field.

Formula

XL = 2πfL

Diode

Definition:

Allows current in one direction.

Formula

Vf ≈ 0.7V
